Who we are

Founded by Shermika Kuweju, a two-decade veteran in the travel industry, SaCeylon Private Limited is fueled by the collective experience of its core team. The company nurtures, trains and makes itself home to some of the finest professionals in the industry, all of whom have a passion for travel and a spirited congeniality, making them your perfect travel consultant. Our team is committed to embody SaCeylon’s standard, by staying on top of market trends and information, while holding ourselves accountable to the highest possible ethical and legal standards, building a solid culture of honesty, integrity, trust and respect. No task is too big or too small for SaCeylon if it means delighting a client.

About Us

SaCeylon Private Limited is an exclusive tour operator in Sri Lanka, offering superior and personalized holidays, both inbound as well as outbound. Our core competency lies in our team’s always current and comprehensive knowledge of what new experiences we can offer our clients, combined with a tacit knowing of how to glean the best fit for them. Thereby, helping them customize a travel plan as unique as they are whilst ensuring they get their money’s worth. Through our strong network of industry partners we can assure clients the best service in the market.

Services

Feel luxurious whilst we do all the hard work for you. We will give you a full service where all your needs are taken care of. Our strong partner network allows us to offer the following services, guaranteeing you, value for money.

Airline ticketing

We are committed to providing you with speedy & comprehensive airline ticketing services consisting of consolidator fares, through our long-standing relationships with all leading inbound and outbound air travel operators of Sri Lanka.

Visa Handling

We provide assistance in obtaining visa for your travels inbound and outbound with the best possible options for flexibility and a hassle free service to you.

Airport transfers and domestic tours

We give you 24x7 access to private, reliable and best value airport transfer services so you can rest easy and feel confident. We are specialized in arranging domestic tours and are well connected to getting you the best offer from our network of service providers.
